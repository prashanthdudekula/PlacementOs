Interview Mastery

üíª Technical Interview Strategy

  • Think Aloud: Explain your thought process to the interviewer.
  • Clarify The Problem: Ask about constraints and edge cases.
  • Brute-Force to Optimal: Start with a simple solution and then optimize.

‚≠ê The STAR Method

Use this for all behavioral questions ("Tell me about a time when...").

  • S - Situation: Describe the context.
  • T - Task: Explain your goal.
  • A - Action: Detail the steps you took.
  • R - Result: Share the quantifiable outcome.
